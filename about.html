Emanuel DeAlba

Founder and Strategic Advisor

Emanuel DeAlba is an MBA graduate, Doctor of Business Administration candidate in Organizational Leadership and Development, United States Air Force veteran, published author, and founder of DeAlba & Sons LLC.

His work blends operational discipline, founder decision-making, AI-enabled analysis, and practical business judgment. DeAlba & Sons was built around a direct belief: founders should validate before they launch, spend, scale, or lock themselves into a weak idea.
