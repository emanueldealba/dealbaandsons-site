Privacy Policy.

Plain-English privacy terms for DeAlba & Sons LLC.

DeAlba & Sons LLC collects information you voluntarily submit through forms, email, or scheduling tools, such as your name, email address, business idea summary, payment status, and decision context.

We use this information to respond to your inquiry, evaluate fit for services, coordinate payment and scheduling, and deliver consulting work if you become a client.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not ask for passwords, unnecessary sensitive information, or payment card details through the website form. Payment card information is processed by Stripe and not stored by DeAlba & Sons LLC.

Website hosting, forms, scheduling, and payment workflows may be processed through service providers used to operate the site, including Netlify, Calendly, Stripe, and similar operational systems.

If you want submitted information removed or corrected, contact emanuel@dealbaandsons.com.

This policy may be updated as the business systems mature.
