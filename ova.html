What you receive

A written decision memo, not a motivational speech.

The Opportunity Viability Assessment pressure-tests the core business logic behind your idea. It is built to help you decide what to do next before you buy assets, hire help, launch ads, or commit to a full build.

  • Clear summary of the business idea and target buyer
  • Demand signal review and market logic check
  • Revenue path and offer clarity assessment
  • Risk, constraint, and assumption map
  • Final recommendation: go, validate-first, pivot, or stop
Best fit

For founders before they spend.

This is for business ideas that are serious enough to examine, but not yet proven enough to scale.

The questions

What the OVA tests.

The diagnostic is built around practical business questions, not vanity planning.

01

Who is the buyer?

We assess whether the customer is specific enough to reach, understand, and sell to.

02

Why now?

We look for urgency, pain, market timing, or operational pressure behind the idea.

03

What could break?

We identify weak assumptions before they become bills, delays, and lost credibility.

Good fit

Use OVA when you are considering:

  • A new business idea
  • A service offer
  • A productized consulting offer
  • A local business launch
  • A major spend before validation
Bad fit

Do not use OVA if:

  • You only want praise
  • You refuse to test the buyer
  • You need a full business plan first
  • You want guaranteed outcomes
  • You want someone to validate an already final decision
