Privacy Policy.

Plain-English privacy terms for DeAlba & Sons LLC.

DeAlba & Sons LLC collects information you voluntarily submit through forms or email, such as your name, email address, business idea summary, and decision context.

We use this information to respond to your inquiry, evaluate fit for services, and deliver consulting work if you become a client.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not ask for passwords, payment card details, or unnecessary sensitive information through the website form.

Website hosting and forms may be processed through Netlify or other service providers used to operate the site. If you want submitted information removed, use the booking page or LinkedIn contact channel until the public business email is finalized.

This policy may be updated as the business systems mature.
