How the work happens

A disciplined path from idea to decision.

The process is built to protect your judgment. Fast enough to move, structured enough to avoid false confidence.

Intake

Submit the idea, buyer, current situation, and proposed next spend.

Clarify

We remove ambiguity around what you are actually trying to sell.

Pressure-test

We examine market logic, buyer behavior, constraints, and risks.

Recommend

You receive a written decision memo with your next best move.

Sequence

If the idea earns commitment, we help define the next practical step.

Operating principle

Do not scale confidence. Scale evidence.

A clean design can make a weak idea feel real. A strong process forces the idea to earn commitment.

What this prevents

  • Spending before the buyer is clear
  • Confusing branding with validation
  • Building workflows around untested assumptions
  • Hiring or buying tools before the model is ready
